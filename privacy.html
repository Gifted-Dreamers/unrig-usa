Privacy Policy

Unrig USA values your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly to us, including your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, zip code, donation information, membership preferences, volunteer interests, and chapter or coalition information.

We may also collect basic information automatically when you use our website, such as your IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, referring pages, and usage data. We may use cookies or similar technologies to help operate the site and understand how visitors use it.

How We Use Information

  • Communicate with you about our mission, events, updates, and actions.
  • Process donations and send receipts or confirmations.
  • Manage memberships, volunteer signups, chapters, and coalition participation.
  • Send emails, texts, and reminders if you choose to receive them.
  • Improve our website and outreach efforts.
  • Meet legal and security obligations.

Sharing Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate the website, process donations, send communications, or manage our systems. We may also share information when required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or the safety of others.

Your Choices

If you sign up for email or text messages, you can opt out at any time. Email messages will include an unsubscribe link. Text messages will include instructions to stop receiving them. You may also contact us to update or delete information we have about you, subject to applicable legal and operational limitations.

Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Children’s Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to make a privacy request, contact us at privacy@unrigusa.org.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
