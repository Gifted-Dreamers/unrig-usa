"...but when a long train of abuses and usurpations, pursuing invariably the same Object, evinces a design to reduce them under absolute Despotism, it is their right, it is their duty, to throw off such Government, and to provide new Guards for their future security." — Declaration of Independence, 1776

A.

Common Sense Anti-Corruption

  • Ban congressional stock trading
  • Close the revolving door between government and lobbying
  • Mandatory financial disclosure for all federal officials
  • Independent ethics enforcement with subpoena power
B.

People's Checks & Balances

  • Citizen-led oversight commissions
  • Recall mechanisms for federal officials
  • Public access to all non-classified federal communications
  • Term limits on long-serving committee chairs
C.

Election Integrity & Security

  • Paper-ballot backups for every federal election
  • End partisan gerrymandering via independent commissions
  • Disclosure for all political advertising — no dark money
  • Protections against voter-roll purges without due process
D.

Equal Justice Under the Law

  • No president is above prosecution for crimes committed in office
  • Bind Supreme Court to a binding code of ethics
  • Whistleblower protections with criminal penalties for retaliation
  • Transparent prosecutorial declination decisions
